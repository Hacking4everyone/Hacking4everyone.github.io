HACKING,HACKERS AND THEIR TYPES

HACKING

We hear that "Nothing is perfect" similarly in the world of hacking we say that "Nothing is secure".
Hacking is the art of finding and exploiting the loopholes in a system.

TYPES OF HACKING

We all think that hacking is illegal.If someone has taken hacking as a profession then he is a criminal. Based on this perception hacking can be divided into two types:

  1. Unethical Hacking
  2. Ethical Hacking
UNETHICAL HACKING

Suppose if a person tries to find and exploit a vulnerability(loopholes) in your system without your permission then this type of hacking is considered as "unethical hacking".

ETHICAL HACKING

Consider the above example.If you hire a hacker and give him permission to find a vulnerability(loopholes) in your system so that you could know how a person who is doing "unethical hacking" can find loopholes such that you could make your system more secure.Then this type of hacking is considered as "ethical hacking".



In short if you find loopholes by taking a legal permission then it is considered as "ethical hacking" and if you do it without legal permission it is considered as "unethical hacking".

HACKERS

Person who performs hacking is known as "HACKER".

TYPES OF HACKERS

Hackers are classified into three categories:


  1. Black Hat Hackers
  2. White Hat Hackers
  3. Grey Hat Hackers
BLACK HAT HACKERS

Person who hacks a system without taking the legal permission and uses the data exploited for his professional benefit then he is considered as black hat hacker.

WHITE HAT HACKERS

Person who make the system more secure by finding a loophole and reporting it to the concerned team.They are also known as Cyber-Security Professionalists.They don't use or reveal the information to any other third party.

GREY HAT HACKERS

These guys are partly black hat and partly white hat hackers.It means that they help the organisation by finding the loopholes in a system to make it more secure but sometimes they indulge in unethical hacking for their personal gains.
